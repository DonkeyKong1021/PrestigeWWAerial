About Andrew Kulas

PrestigeWW Aerial Media is owned and operated solely by Andrew Kulas, who personally handles all drone flying, photography, videography, and editing work.

My journey began with a DJI Mini 3 drone and a passion for capturing unique perspectives. I specialize in professional drone photography and immersive 360° virtual tours that transform how properties and spaces are showcased.

As a one-person operation, I'm able to provide personalized service and attention to detail on every project. I work directly with real estate professionals and property developers to create visual content that truly stands out in a competitive market.
