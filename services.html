Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about drone photography services

How quickly can I get my photos/videos after the shoot?

For standard projects, I typically deliver edited photos within 48 hours and video content within 3-5 business days, depending on complexity. If you need a faster turnaround, just let me know and I'll do my best to accommodate your timeline.

What areas do you service in Virginia?

I'm based in Richmond and primarily serve the greater Richmond area, including Henrico, Chesterfield, and Hanover counties. I'm also available for projects throughout Central Virginia. For locations further away, travel fees may apply.

What happens if the weather is bad on our scheduled day?

Safety and image quality are my priorities. If weather conditions are unsuitable for drone operations (rain, high winds, etc.), I'll work with you to reschedule at no additional cost. I monitor forecasts closely and will communicate with you 24-48 hours in advance if rescheduling appears necessary.

Are you FAA certified and insured?

Yes, I am an FAA-licensed Part 107 certified drone pilot and carry full liability insurance coverage. I strictly adhere to all FAA regulations and can obtain special authorizations for controlled airspace when needed for your project.

How do I receive my finished files?

All deliverables are provided through a secure digital download link. You'll receive high-resolution images and videos optimized for both web use and printing. Files are delivered in industry-standard formats (JPG, MP4, etc.) that work across all platforms and devices.
