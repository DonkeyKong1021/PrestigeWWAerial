Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my final deliverables?

All deliverables are provided through a secure digital download link. High-resolution images, virtual tours, and videos are optimized for both web use and printing. We retain backups of all client projects for 90 days after delivery.

What areas do you service?

I primarily serve Richmond, Virginia and the surrounding areas. I'm also available for projects throughout Virginia and neighboring states (North Carolina, Maryland, West Virginia, Kentucky, Tennessee, and Washington DC). For projects beyond these areas, please contact me for availability and potential travel arrangements.

How quickly can I get my project completed?

Typical turnaround time is 2-3 business days for standard projects. Express delivery options are available for an additional fee. Complex projects or those requiring extensive editing may take longer. I'll always provide you with a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

What if the weather is bad on my scheduled shooting day?

Safety and quality are my top priorities. Virginia weather can be unpredictable, with summer thunderstorms and winter weather events. If conditions make flying unsafe or would compromise image quality, I'll work with you to reschedule at no additional cost. I monitor weather forecasts closely and will communicate with you 24-48 hours in advance if rescheduling appears necessary.
